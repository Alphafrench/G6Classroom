## General Questions

### What is the Educational Platform?
The Educational Platform is a comprehensive learning management system designed to support online, hybrid, and traditional classroom learning. It provides tools for course management, assignment submission, grade tracking, communication, and administrative oversight.

### Who can use the platform?
The platform serves multiple user types:
- **Students**: Access courses, submit assignments, track grades
- **Teachers**: Create courses, manage content, grade work, track attendance
- **Administrators**: Manage users, configure system, generate reports
- **Support Staff**: Assist with technical and academic support

### Is there a cost to use the platform?
The platform is typically provided by your institution. Contact your educational institution's IT department or administration for specific cost information.

### What devices and browsers are supported?
**Desktop Browsers:**
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

**Mobile Devices:**
- iOS 12+ (iPhone and iPad)
- Android 8+
- Tablets and smartphones

## Getting Started

### How do I log in for the first time?
1. Go to your institution's platform URL
2. Use the credentials provided by your institution
3. You may be prompted to change your password
4. Complete any required profile information

### I can't remember my password. What should I do?
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. If you don't receive an email, contact IT support

### Where can I find my courses?
After logging in:
- Students: Check your "My Courses" section or dashboard
- Teachers: Navigate to "My Courses" to see assigned courses
- Your most recent courses appear on the main dashboard

### How do I update my profile?
1. Click your profile picture or name in the top-right corner
2. Select "Edit Profile" or "Account Settings"
3. Update your information and save changes

## Account and Access

### Can I access the platform from multiple devices?
Yes! You can log in from any device with internet access. Your data syncs automatically across devices.

### What should I do if I can't log in?
1. Verify your username and password
2. Check if your account is active
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact IT support if problems persist

### How do I change my password?
1. Go to your profile settings
2. Select "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Create a new password that meets requirements
5. Confirm the new password

### Why am I seeing an "access denied" message?
This can happen if:
- Your account has been deactivated
- You don't have permission for that content
- You're trying to access a course you haven't enrolled in
- There are technical issues - try refreshing the page

### Can I use the platform without internet?
Limited offline access is available:
- Downloaded course materials can be viewed offline
- Some assignments can be worked on offline and submitted when connected
- Synchronization happens when you reconnect to the internet

## Courses and Content

### How do I enroll in a course?
**Self-Enrollment:**
1. Go to the course catalog
2. Find the course you want
3. Click "Enroll" or "Register"
4. Follow any additional steps required

**Instructor Approval Required:**
1. Submit enrollment request
2. Wait for instructor approval
3. You'll be notified when approved

### When do course materials become available?
Materials availability depends on:
- Course start date
- Instructor settings
- Your enrollment date
- Module release schedule

### How do I download course materials?
1. Navigate to the course content section
2. Find the file you want to download
3. Click the download link or right-click and "Save As"
4. The file will download to your device

### What file formats are supported for course materials?
Common formats include:
- **Documents**: PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX
- **Images**: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG
- **Videos**: MP4, AVI, MOV
- **Audio**: MP3, WAV, AAC

### Can I print course materials?
Yes! Most materials can be printed directly from your browser or downloaded for printing.

### How do I find specific content in a course?
- Use the search function within the course
- Navigate through the course outline/table of contents
- Check the course announcement section
- Contact your instructor for guidance

## Assignments and Grading

### How do I submit an assignment?
1. Go to the assignment page
2. Read the instructions carefully
3. Complete your work
4. Click "Submit Assignment"
5. Upload your file or enter text
6. Add comments if required
7. Click "Submit" to finalize

### What file types can I submit?
Common submission formats:
- PDF, DOC, DOCX (written work)
- PPT, PPTX (presentations)
- JPG, PNG (images)
- MP4, MOV (videos)
- ZIP (multiple files)

### Is there a file size limit?
Yes, typically:
- Maximum file size: 50MB per submission
- Multiple files: Some assignments allow multiple uploads
- Check assignment instructions for specific limits

### Can I resubmit an assignment?
This depends on your instructor's settings:
- Some allow multiple submissions
- Others only accept one submission
- Late submissions may not be allowed
- Check the assignment instructions or ask your instructor

### How will I know when my assignment is graded?
You'll receive notifications when:
- Your assignment is graded (email or platform notification)
- New feedback is available
- Your grade is posted
- Check the Grades section for updated scores

### When will I receive my grades?
Grading times vary by instructor:
- Some grades within a few days
- Others may take several weeks
- Check your course syllabus for grading timelines
- Contact your instructor if grades are significantly delayed

### How do I view my instructor's feedback?
1. Go to the assignment submission page
2. Look for "Grade" or "Feedback" section
3. Read written comments
4. Download any annotated files
5. Review rubric scores if provided

### What does "Draft" status mean?
"Draft" means:
- Your work has been saved but not submitted
- Only you can see the draft
- You can continue editing
- Submit before the deadline to complete

### Can I submit assignments after the deadline?
**Late submissions** depend on your instructor's policy:
- Some allow late submissions with penalty
- Others have strict no-late-work policies
- Check your course syllabus or ask your instructor
- Some may offer grace periods

### How does attendance affect my grade?
Attendance impact varies by course:

- Check your course syllabus for specific policies

### Can I make up missed classes?
Make-up opportunities depend on:
- Course policy
- Reason for absence
- Instructor discretion
- Available make-up activities

## Technical Support

### Who can help me with technical problems?
**Level 1 - Self-Help:**
- Check this FAQ
- Review platform documentation
- Try troubleshooting steps

**Level 2 - Institutional Support:**
- IT Help Desk
- Technical support staff
- Campus technology centers

**Level 3 - Platform Support:**
- Platform vendor support
- System administrators
- Emergency technical support

### What information should I provide when reporting a problem?
**Essential Details:**
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Any error messages you saw
- What browser you're using
- What device you're on

**Additional Helpful Information:**
- Screenshots of the problem
- Time when the problem occurred
- Steps you've already tried
- Your user role (student, teacher, etc.)

### The platform is running slowly. What can I do?
**Immediate Solutions:**
1. Refresh your browser (F5)
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Close other browser tabs
4. Check your internet connection

**If Problems Persist:**
- Try a different browser
- Restart your device
- Contact your IT support team

### Why can't I upload my assignment file?
**Common Issues:**
- File is too large (check size limit)
- File type not supported
- Internet connection problems
- Browser compatibility issues

**Solutions:**
- Check assignment instructions for file requirements
- Try a different browser
- Compress large files or break into smaller parts
- Ensure stable internet connection

### My work disappeared. Can it be recovered?
**Auto-Save Features:**
- Platform may have auto-saved your work
- Check for drafts in assignment submissions
- Look in browser's temporary files

**Prevention Tips:**
- Save work frequently
- Keep backup copies
- Submit work before making major changes
- Use cloud storage for important files

## Privacy and Security

### How is my personal information protected?
**Security Measures:**
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and permissions
- Privacy-compliant data handling

**Your Responsibilities:**
- Keep your password secure
- Don't share your login credentials
- Log out from shared computers
- Report suspicious activity

### Who can see my grades and personal information?
**Visibility Rules:**
- Only you and your instructors can see your grades
- Administrators can see course-wide statistics
- Parents/guardians may have access (if permitted by institution)
- Other students cannot see your personal information

### Can other students see my submissions?
No! Assignment submissions are private between you and your instructor, unless:
- Your instructor specifically allows peer review
- It's a group assignment
- You choose to share your work

### How do I report a security concern?
**Immediate Actions:**
- Don't share sensitive information further
- Change your password immediately
- Log out from all devices

**Report To:**
- IT Security team at your institution
- Platform administrator
- Use the platform's security reporting feature

### What should I do if I think someone accessed my account?
1. Change your password immediately
2. Check your account for unauthorized changes
3. Contact IT support to report the incident
4. Monitor your account for unusual activity
5. Consider enabling two-factor authentication

## Troubleshooting

### I can't see my course. Where did it go?
**Possible Reasons:**
- Course hasn't started yet
- You're not enrolled
- Technical issue - try refreshing
- Course has ended

**Solutions:**
- Check course start date
- Verify enrollment status
- Contact your instructor
- Try a different browser

### My assignment won't submit. What should I do?
**Troubleshooting Steps:**
1. Check file size and format
2. Ensure stable internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Save as different file format
5. Contact support if problem persists

### The gradebook shows incorrect grades. How do I fix this?
1. First, refresh your browser
2. Check if instructor has posted all grades
3. Contact your instructor for clarification
4. If there's clearly an error, request grade review

### I received an error message. What does it mean?
**Common Error Messages and Solutions:**

**"Session Expired"**
- Log out and log back in
- Save work more frequently

**"Access Denied"**
- Check your permissions
- Contact your instructor

**"File Too Large"**
- Compress your file
- Submit in smaller parts

**"Invalid File Type"**
- Check assignment instructions
- Convert file to supported format

### The platform keeps logging me out. Why?
**Possible Causes:**
- Session timeout settings
- Browser cookies disabled
- Multiple browser windows open
- Security settings

**Solutions:**
- Enable cookies
- Close unnecessary tabs
- Check browser settings
- Contact IT support

### Mobile app keeps crashing. What should I do?
1. Update the app to latest version
2. Restart your device
3. Clear app cache and data
4. Check available storage space
5. Reinstall the app if necessary

### I can't hear audio in course videos. How do I fix this?
**Check These Settings:**
- Device volume and mute settings
- Browser audio permissions
- Video player controls
- Headphone connections
- Try different audio source

### Discussion forum posts aren't appearing. Why?
**Possible Reasons:**
- Posts awaiting moderation
- Technical synchronization delay
- Browser cache issues
- Network connection problems

**Solutions:**
- Wait a few minutes
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support if posts don't appear

### Calendar events are missing. How do I fix this?
1. Refresh your calendar view
2. Check if events are filtered out
3. Verify your timezone settings
4. Sync with external calendar
5. Contact support if issues persist
